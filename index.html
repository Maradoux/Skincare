WEEKS 5-8 (Days 29-60) - Continue Same Pattern
Weekly Pattern to Repeat:
• Monday, Tuesday, Friday, Saturday: Azelaic Acid evenings
• Wednesday: Optional Kojic Acid evening (2-3x per week)
• Thursday & Sunday: Glycolic Acid exfoliation nights
• Every Morning: Cleanser → Snail Mucin → Alpha Arbutin → Moisturizer → Sunscreen SPF 50

Expected Results by Week 8: Even skin tone, reduced pigmentation around eyes and mouth, smoother texture, brighter overall complexion

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • NEVER skip sunscreen - Apply every morning, even indoors. Without it, pigmentation won't fade.
  • Don't layer strong actives - Never use Kojic + Glycolic + Azelaic on the same night.
  • Patch test first - Test new products on a small area before full application.
  • If irritation occurs - Reduce frequency instead of stopping completely.
  • Stay consistent - Results take 4-8 weeks. Trust the process!

📅 Expected Timeline

  • Weeks 1-2: More hydrated skin, possible purging or slight dryness (normal when introducing acids)
  • Weeks 3-4: Brighter overall skin tone, subtle fading of newer dark marks
  • Weeks 5-8: More even tone, reduced pigmentation around mouth/eyes, noticeably smoother skin
